## üí° TIPS FOR SUCCESSFUL SUBMISSION

### App Store (iOS)
1. **Be thorough** - Fill out all fields completely
2. **Quality screenshots** - Show actual app interface, no mockups
3. **Clear description** - Focus on features, not marketing hype
4. **Privacy first** - Clearly explain data usage
5. **Test thoroughly** - No crashes or major bugs
6. **Respond quickly** - If rejected, fix and resubmit ASAP

### Play Store (Android)
1. **Data safety** - Be honest about data collection
2. **Content rating** - Answer questions accurately
3. **Target API** - Always target latest Android version
4. **AAB format** - Use App Bundles, not APKs
5. **Gradual rollout** - Consider starting with 10-20% rollout
6. **Quality guidelines** - Follow Material Design principles
