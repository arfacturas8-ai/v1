## Getting Started

CRYB Platform is accessible through multiple channels:
- **Web App**: https://platform.cryb.ai
- **Mobile App**: Available for iOS and Android
- **Admin Panel**: https://admin.cryb.ai (for moderators and admins)

### System Requirements

**Web Browser:**
- Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+
- JavaScript enabled
- WebSocket support for real-time features

**Mobile:**
- iOS 13+ or Android 8.0+
- 100MB free storage space
- Internet connection (3G or better recommended)

## Creating an Account

### Registration Process

1. **Visit the Platform**: Go to https://platform.cryb.ai
2. **Click "Sign Up"**: Located in the top-right corner
3. **Choose Registration Method**:
   - Email and password
   - Google OAuth
   - Discord OAuth
   - GitHub OAuth

### Email Registration

1. **Enter Details**:
   - **Username**: 3-20 characters, letters, numbers, and underscores only
   - **Email**: Valid email address for verification
   - **Password**: Minimum 8 characters with uppercase, lowercase, and numbers
   - **Display Name**: How your name appears to other users

2. **Verify Email**: Check your inbox for a verification link and click it

3. **Complete Profile**: Add optional information:
   - Profile picture
   - Bio description
   - Location
   - Website/social links

### OAuth Registration

1. **Select Provider**: Click on Google, Discord, or GitHub
2. **Authorize**: Grant permissions on the OAuth provider
3. **Complete Profile**: Fill in any missing information
4. **Link Additional Accounts**: Connect other OAuth providers if desired

### Account Security

- **Two-Factor Authentication**: Enable 2FA in settings for enhanced security
- **Strong Password**: Use a unique, strong password
- **Recovery Email**: Add a backup email for account recovery
- **Login Alerts**: Monitor login notifications for suspicious activity

## Joining Communities

Communities are the heart of CRYB Platform, similar to Discord servers or Reddit subreddits.

### Finding Communities

1. **Browse Popular**: Check the homepage for trending communities
2. **Search**: Use the search bar to find specific topics
3. **Categories**: Browse by category (Gaming, Technology, Art, etc.)
4. **Recommendations**: View personalized suggestions based on your interests

### Joining a Community

1. **Visit Community Page**: Click on any community from search or browse
2. **Review Information**:
   - Community description and rules
   - Member count and activity level
   - Recent posts and discussions
3. **Click "Join"**: Request to join (some communities require approval)
4. **Read Rules**: Familiarize yourself with community guidelines

### Community Features

**Public Communities**: Open to all users
**Private Communities**: Require invitation or approval
**Verified Communities**: Official communities with verified badges

### Community Roles and Permissions

- **Member**: Basic participation rights
- **Contributor**: Enhanced posting privileges
- **Moderator**: Content moderation abilities
- **Admin**: Full community management access

## Creating Posts and Comments

CRYB supports Reddit-style posts with rich content and interactive features.

### Creating a Post

1. **Navigate to Community**: Join a community first
2. **Click "Create Post"**: Located in the community sidebar
3. **Choose Post Type**:
   - **Text Post**: Text content with markdown support
   - **Link Post**: Share external links with preview
   - **Image Post**: Upload images (max 10MB each)
   - **Video Post**: Upload videos (max 100MB)
   - **Poll Post**: Create interactive polls

### Post Content Guidelines

**Text Posts:**
- Support Markdown formatting
- Maximum 40,000 characters
- Can include code blocks, lists, and formatting

**Image Posts:**
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, WEBP
- Maximum file size: 10MB per image
- Up to 20 images per post
- Automatic image optimization

**Video Posts:**
- Supported formats: MP4, MOV, AVI, WEBM
- Maximum file size: 100MB
- Automatic video transcoding
- Thumbnail generation

### Post Features

**Voting System:**
- Upvote/downvote posts and comments
- Karma points based on community reception
- Sort by Hot, New, Top, Rising

**Sharing:**
- Copy link to post
- Share to external platforms
- Cross-post to other communities

**Save and Collections:**
- Save posts for later reading
- Organize saved content in collections
- Export saved content

### Commenting

1. **Click "Comment"**: On any post
2. **Write Your Comment**: Supports markdown and mentions
3. **Add Media**: Include images or GIFs in comments
4. **Submit**: Your comment appears in real-time

**Comment Features:**
- **Threaded Discussions**: Reply to specific comments
- **Mention Users**: Use @username to notify users
- **Edit History**: See edit timestamps and history
- **Moderation Tools**: Report inappropriate content

### Content Moderation

**AI Moderation:**
- Automatic detection of spam and inappropriate content
- NSFW content detection and flagging
- Toxicity analysis and warnings

**Community Moderation:**
- Report posts and comments
- Community-specific rules enforcement
- Moderator review process

## Voice and Video Calls

CRYB integrates LiveKit for high-quality voice and video communication.

### Voice Channels

**Joining Voice:**
1. **Click Voice Channel**: In community sidebar
2. **Grant Permissions**: Allow microphone access
3. **Adjust Settings**: Set input/output devices
4. **Start Talking**: Voice activation or push-to-talk

**Voice Features:**
- **High-Quality Audio**: Opus codec for crystal-clear voice
- **Noise Suppression**: AI-powered background noise removal
- **Echo Cancellation**: Automatic echo and feedback elimination
- **Server-Side Recording**: Optional voice channel recording

### Video Calls

**Starting Video:**
1. **Join Voice Channel**: Must be in voice first
2. **Enable Camera**: Click camera icon
3. **Grant Permissions**: Allow camera access
4. **Adjust Settings**: Set video quality and effects

**Video Features:**
- **HD Video**: Up to 1080p video quality
- **Screen Sharing**: Share your screen or specific applications
- **Virtual Backgrounds**: Custom backgrounds and blur effects
- **Picture-in-Picture**: Minimize video while using other features

### Call Management

**Participant Controls:**
- Mute/unmute yourself
- Turn camera on/off
- Adjust individual user volumes
- View participant list

**Host Controls** (for moderators):
- Mute all participants
- Remove disruptive users
- Record calls (with consent)
- Manage permissions

### Voice/Video Settings

**Audio Settings:**
- Input/output device selection
- Microphone sensitivity
- Noise suppression levels
- Audio quality preferences

**Video Settings:**
- Camera selection
- Video resolution (480p, 720p, 1080p)
- Frame rate optimization
- Bandwidth management

### Troubleshooting Voice/Video

**Common Issues:**
- **No Audio**: Check microphone permissions and device selection
- **Echo**: Enable echo cancellation in settings
- **Poor Quality**: Reduce video resolution or check internet connection
- **Connection Issues**: Try refreshing or rejoining the channel

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is CRYB Platform free to use?**
A: Yes, CRYB Platform is free to use with all core features available. Premium features may be introduced in the future.

**Q: Can I use CRYB on mobile devices?**
A: Yes, CRYB is available as a mobile app for iOS and Android, plus the web version works great on mobile browsers.

**Q: How do I report inappropriate content?**
A: Click the report button on any post, comment, or message. Our moderation team reviews all reports within 24 hours.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Yes, you can delete your account from Settings > Account > Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What browsers support CRYB?**
A: CRYB works best on Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, and Edge 90+. WebSocket support is required for real-time features.

**Q: Why can't I hear audio in voice channels?**
A: Check your browser permissions, audio device settings, and ensure your microphone/speakers are properly configured.

**Q: How large files can I upload?**
A: Images up to 10MB, videos up to 100MB, and other files up to 25MB are supported.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption, secure data centers, and follow best practices for data protection.

### Community Questions

**Q: How do I become a moderator?**
A: Community owners can promote active, trusted members to moderator status. There's no application process.

**Q: Can I create my own community?**
A: Yes, any user can create a new community. Visit the Communities page and click "Create Community."

**Q: What happens if I violate community rules?**
A: Violations may result in warnings, temporary restrictions, or permanent bans depending on severity.

**Q: How do I appeal a ban or restriction?**
A: Contact the community moderators or use the appeal system in your account settings.

### Feature Questions

**Q: How does the karma system work?**
A: Karma is calculated based on upvotes and downvotes on your posts and comments. It reflects community appreciation for your contributions.

**Q: Can I edit posts and comments after posting?**
A: Yes, you can edit your content. Edits are marked with timestamps to maintain transparency.

**Q: How do I use markdown formatting?**
A: CRYB supports standard markdown: **bold**, *italic*, `code`, > quotes, and more. There's a formatting guide in the editor.

**Q: Can I schedule posts?**
A: Post scheduling is planned for a future update. Currently, all posts are published immediately.

### Privacy Questions

**Q: Who can see my personal information?**
A: Only information you choose to make public is visible to others. You control all privacy settings in your account.

**Q: Do you sell my data to third parties?**
A: No, we never sell user data. We may use anonymized, aggregated data for platform improvements.

**Q: How long do you keep my data?**
A: Active account data is kept indefinitely. Deleted accounts are purged after 90 days, except for legal compliance requirements.

**Q: Can I download my data?**
A: Yes, you can download all your data in JSON format from Settings > Privacy > Data Export.
